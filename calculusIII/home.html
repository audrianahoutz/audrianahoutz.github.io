Calculus III

Welcome to Calculus III, an advanced exploration of multivariable calculus, including an essential exploration of vector calculus. This course builds on the foundational concepts from Calculus I and II, guiding you through functions of multiple variables, partial derivatives, and multiple integrals. A significant portion is dedicated to vector calculusâ€”vector fields, line integrals, surface integrals, and the central theorems of Green, Stokes, and Gauss. These areas are crucial for applying mathematical theory to real-world problems in engineering, physics, and other disciplines. With a blend of theoretical exploration and practical application, this course is designed to deepen your understanding of the multi-dimensional mathematical universe. Prerequisites include a solid understanding of single-variable calculus. Over the semester, we will cover a comprehensive list of 22 topics:
