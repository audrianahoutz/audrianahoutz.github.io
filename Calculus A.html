About Calculus A at Notre Dame:

Calculus A is the first course in the calculus sequence at the University of Notre Dame. It covers fundamental concepts of differential and integral calculus, focusing on understanding limits, derivatives, and the basics of integration. This course provides essential mathematical tools that are crucial for further studies in mathematics, science, and engineering.
