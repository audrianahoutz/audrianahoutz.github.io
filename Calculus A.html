Welcome to the Calculus A Course Webpage! This site will serve as a central hub for all course-related materials, including detailed lecture notes, homework assignments, important announcements, and additional resources to help you succeed in Calculus A. About Calculus A at Notre Dame: Calculus A is the first course in the calculus sequence at the University of Notre Dame. It covers fundamental concepts of differential and integral calculus, focusing on understanding limits, derivatives, and the basics of integration. This course provides essential mathematical tools that are crucial for further studies in mathematics, science, and engineering. Please check this webpage regularly for updates and information related to our course.
